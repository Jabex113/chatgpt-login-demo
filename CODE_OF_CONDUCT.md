# Code of Conduct

Use practical, respectful communication.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Keep feedback about the work, not the person.
- Share enough context for maintainers and contributors to reproduce issues.
- Avoid posting secrets, account identifiers, private chat logs, or screenshots that expose personal data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing another person’s private information.
- Submitting malicious code or instructions designed to steal tokens, cookies, or account access.
- Ignoring maintainer decisions after a clear explanation has been given.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and pull requests that violate this policy. Repeat or severe violations may lead to a ban from the project.
